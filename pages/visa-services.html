Get a Visa For

United Kingdom (UK)

Tourist | Visit | Study | Business

Checklist:

Valid passport, photos, proof of funds, itinerary, invitation (if applicable), employer/student letters.

Our Role:

Visa type guidance, form support, document review, biometrics appointment booking, interview prep.

Schengen Europe

Tourist | Visit | Business

Checklist:

Application form, passport, travel insurance (€30,000+), flight & hotel bookings, bank statements, cover letter.

Our Role:

Embassy routing, document guidance, VFS appointment support, interview preparation.

UAE / Dubai

Tourist | Visit | Business

Options:

30/60/90 days, single or multiple entry.

Our Role:

Eligibility confirmation, online submission through approved channels, tracking & updates.

Notes:

No embassy interview required — eVisa issued electronically.

United States

B1/B2 Tourist/Business | F-1 Study

Checklist:

DS-160 form, passport, financial proof, itinerary, SEVIS (for students).

Our Role:

Form assistance, MRV fee and appointment booking, interview prep.

Notes:

Interview mandatory at US Embassy Nairobi — we help you prepare confidently.

Canada

Visitor | Study | Work

Checklist:

Passport, forms via IRCC portal, biometrics, financial proof, invitation letters (if required).

Our Role:

IRCC portal support, checklist preparation, VAC/VFS handling.

❓ FAQs

Is a visa guaranteed?

+

No. Embassies/consulates make the final decision. Our role is to maximize your chances by submitting accurate and complete applications.

How long does it take?

+

Varies by country — UK (2–6 weeks), Schengen (15–21 days), US (depends on appointment availability), Canada (varies).

Do you book appointments?

+

Yes — we help secure your slot at embassies, consulates, or VFS centers.
