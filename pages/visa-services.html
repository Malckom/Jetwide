VISA SERVICES — SIMPLE, GUIDED, RELIABLE

We assist our Clients in the Visa Application process and make sure it runs smoothly. Tourist, business, study & family visas made easier for Kenyan travelers.

📞 Contact Us

Planning a holiday, business trip, studies, or a family visit abroad? Jetwide's Visa Desk helps you choose the right visa, prepare documents correctly, book appointments, and avoid common mistakes. We don't issue visas—embassies/consulates decide—but we make the process clear, organized, and stress-free.

IMPORTANT
Government Embassies/VFS fees are paid directly to official channels.
Jetwide service fees are separate and cover consultation, document guidance, appointment support, and interview prep (where applicable).
Processing times vary by country and season; we'll advise current ranges once consulted.

How it works

• Pre-Check & Consult —
We confirm the correct visa type and give you a tailored checklist.
• Document Prep —
Forms, photos, itinerary, proof of funds, insurance, invitations (if any).
• Submission & Appointment —
Embassy/consulate/VFS submission or online portal; biometrics where required.
• Decision & Passport —
We guide tracking & collection. Outcome is at the sole discretion of the issuing authority.
