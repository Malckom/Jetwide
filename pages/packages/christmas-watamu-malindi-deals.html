CHRISTMAS COASTAL DEALS - Watamu & Malindi

FROM KSh 95,835
DURATION 5 Nights
TRAVEL PERIOD 21 Dec - 3 Jan
DESTINATION Watamu/Malindi, Kenya

Package Overview

Celebrate Christmas and New Year in paradise! Escape to the pristine beaches of Watamu & Malindi for an unforgettable 5-night festive experience. Enjoy world-class marine parks, boutique resorts, and exclusive beach experiences while celebrating the holidays in one of Kenya's most beautiful coastal destinations.

📍 Location: Watamu & Malindi, Kenya
⏰ Travel Period: 21st December - 3rd January
🚆 Transport: Return SGR + Watamu/Malindi transfers

Package Highlights

  • 5 nights accommodation at exclusive boutique resorts
  • Christmas Eve gala dinners and New Year's celebrations
  • Meals as per hotel meal plan (BB, Half Board, Full Board, or All-Inclusive)
  • Return SGR train tickets from Nairobi to Mombasa
  • Comfortable road transfers to Watamu/Malindi (~2 hours)
  • Access to UNESCO Marine National Park
  • Special festive entertainment and activities

Why Celebrate in Watamu & Malindi?

  • Exclusive Experience: Boutique properties with personalized festive programs
  • Marine Paradise: World-class snorkeling and diving in protected waters
  • Intimate Celebrations: Smaller resorts mean more personalized holiday experiences
  • Pristine Beaches: Less crowded than Mombasa with powder-white sand
  • Unique Activities: Turtle watching, bio-luminescent plankton tours, Gede Ruins

Festive Season Activities

  • Christmas Eve gala dinner with live entertainment
  • New Year's Eve beach parties and fireworks
  • Snorkeling and diving in Watamu Marine Park
  • Sea turtle conservation experiences
  • Deep-sea fishing expeditions
  • Kite surfing and water sports
  • Mida Creek kayaking adventures
  • Italian-Swahili fusion holiday dining
  • Dhow cruises with festive themes

Important Notes

  • Prices are per person sharing (double occupancy)
  • Single room supplement available on request
  • Child rates available - contact us for details
  • Valid for travel between 21st December and 3rd January
  • Minimum stay: 5 nights during peak season
  • Full payment required 14 days before travel
  • Marine park fees not included (approx. $20 per person)
  • Festive supplements included in the quoted rates
