Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai

Meals: None | Overnight: Dubai Hotel

Arrive at Dubai International Airport and meet your tour representative. Transfer to your hotel for check-in. Rest of the day at leisure to relax or explore the nearby area. In the evening, optional visit to Dubai Mall and witness the spectacular Dubai Fountain show (every 30 minutes from 6 PM).

Optional evening activity: Dubai Mall & Fountain Show

Day 2: Dubai City Tour & Burj Khalifa

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Dubai Hotel

After breakfast, embark on a half-day Dubai city tour. Visit the iconic Jumeirah Mosque, drive along Jumeirah Beach with photo stops, and see the magnificent Burj Al Arab hotel. Continue to the historic Al Fahidi neighborhood and visit Dubai Museum. Cross Dubai Creek by traditional Abra boat. Explore the vibrant Gold Souk and Spice Souk in Deira.

In the afternoon, visit the world's tallest building - Burj Khalifa. Take the high-speed elevator to the observation deck on the 124th and 125th floors for breathtaking 360-degree views of Dubai's skyline. Spend the evening at Dubai Mall for shopping and dining.

Day 3: Desert Safari Adventure

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner | Overnight: Dubai Hotel

Enjoy breakfast and a leisurely morning at your hotel. Optional activities include visiting IMG Worlds of Adventure, Dubai Miracle Garden, or relaxing at the beach.

In the afternoon (around 3:30 PM), get picked up for an unforgettable desert safari experience. Enjoy thrilling dune bashing in 4x4 vehicles, try sandboarding, and take stunning sunset photos in the Arabian desert. Arrive at a traditional Bedouin-style camp for a delicious BBQ dinner with live entertainment including belly dancing, Tanoura dance, and fire shows. Enjoy henna painting, shisha smoking, and stargazing before returning to your hotel.

Day 4: Dubai Marina & Palm Jumeirah

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner | Overnight: Dubai Hotel

After breakfast, visit Palm Jumeirah, the world's largest man-made island. Take the monorail to Atlantis The Palm for photo opportunities. Explore Dubai Marina, one of the world's largest and most impressive waterfront developments. Walk along the Marina Walk promenade with its cafes, restaurants, and stunning yacht views.

In the evening, embark on a romantic Dubai Marina dhow cruise. Enjoy a delicious international buffet dinner while cruising along the stunning Marina skyline. Experience the blend of traditional Arabian dhow and modern architecture as you sail past iconic landmarks beautifully lit up at night.

Day 5: Leisure & Departure

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: N/A

Enjoy your final breakfast in Dubai. Depending on your flight time, you may have some last-minute shopping at Dubai Mall or visit the nearby Souk Al Bahar. Check out from your hotel and transfer to Dubai International Airport for your flight back to Nairobi, carrying wonderful memories of your Dubai honeymoon.

Optional: Late checkout available at extra cost (subject to availability)
