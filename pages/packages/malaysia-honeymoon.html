Malaysia Honeymoon Package

Experience the perfect honeymoon combining the vibrant city life of Kuala Lumpur, the pristine beaches of Langkawi, and the cultural richness of Penang. This 7-day romantic getaway is designed for couples seeking adventure, relaxation, and unforgettable memories.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kuala Lumpur

Arrive at Kuala Lumpur International Airport. Meet and greet by our representative. Transfer to your hotel in the city center.

  • Check-in at Grand Millennium Hotel
  • Evening at leisure to explore Bukit Bintang
  • Welcome dinner at hotel restaurant

Meals: Dinner | Overnight: Kuala Lumpur

Day 2: Kuala Lumpur City Tour

Full day exploring the highlights of Malaysia's capital city.

  • Visit Petronas Twin Towers & KLCC Park
  • Tour of National Mosque & Independence Square
  • Visit Batu Caves
  • Shopping at Central Market
  • Evening at KL Tower for panoramic views

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Kuala Lumpur

Day 3: Kuala Lumpur to Langkawi

Morning flight to Langkawi, the jewel of Kedah.

  • Transfer to Meritus Pelangi Beach Resort
  • Afternoon at leisure on private beach
  • Sunset cruise (optional)
  • Romantic beachfront dinner

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner | Overnight: Langkawi

Day 4: Langkawi Island Tour

Discover the natural beauty of Langkawi.

  • Cable car ride to Mount Machinchang
  • Walk on the Sky Bridge
  • Visit Eagle Square & Legend Park
  • Underwater World aquarium
  • Evening free for spa treatments

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Langkawi

Day 5: Langkawi to Penang

Ferry transfer to the Pearl of the Orient.

  • Scenic ferry ride to Penang
  • Check-in at Eastern & Oriental Hotel
  • Afternoon heritage walk in Georgetown
  • Street art exploration
  • Dinner at Gurney Drive hawker center

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Penang

Day 6: Penang Heritage & Culture

Explore UNESCO World Heritage sites.

  • Visit Kek Lok Si Temple
  • Penang Hill funicular railway
  • Clan Jetties exploration
  • Peranakan Mansion tour
  • Cooking class experience
  • Farewell dinner at rooftop restaurant

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner | Overnight: Penang

Day 7: Departure

Final day in paradise.

  • Leisurely breakfast
  • Last-minute shopping at Queensbay Mall
  • Transfer to Penang International Airport
  • Depart for Nairobi with beautiful memories

Meals: Breakfast
