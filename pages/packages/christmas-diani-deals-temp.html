CHRISTMAS NORTHCOAST DEALS - Mombasa

FROM KSh 63,720
DURATION 5 Nights
TRAVEL PERIOD 21 Dec 2025 - 3 Jan 2026
DESTINATION Mombasa, Kenya

Package Overview

Celebrate Christmas and New Year on Kenya's stunning North Coast! This 5-night festive season package offers the perfect holiday escape with a choice of 12 exceptional beachfront hotels. Enjoy the festive atmosphere, special holiday entertainment, and create unforgettable memories as you ring in the New Year by the Indian Ocean.

📍 Location: Mombasa North Coast, Kenya
⏰ Travel Period: 21st December 2025 - 3rd January 2026
🚆 Transport: Return SGR (Standard Gauge Railway) from Nairobi

Package Highlights

  • 5 nights accommodation at your choice of hotel
  • Meals as per hotel meal plan (Half Board, Full Board, or All-Inclusive)
  • Return SGR train tickets from Nairobi to Mombasa
  • SGR transfers in Mombasa to your hotel
  • Special Christmas and New Year festivities at hotels
  • Access to pristine North Coast beaches
  • Festive entertainment and themed dinners
  • 12 hotel options to suit every budget

Festive Season Experience

  • Christmas Celebrations: Special Christmas Eve and Day programs
  • New Year's Eve: Gala dinners and countdown parties
  • Live Entertainment: Music, cultural shows, and performances
  • Themed Dining: Special festive menus and buffets
  • Family Activities: Kids' clubs with holiday-themed programs
  • Beach Activities: Water sports, beach games, and competitions

Activities & Attractions

  • Swimming in the warm Indian Ocean waters
  • Snorkeling and diving at nearby coral reefs
  • Water sports: jet skiing, windsurfing, kayaking
  • Visit Fort Jesus (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Explore Old Town Mombasa
  • Haller Park wildlife sanctuary
  • Bamburi Beach and Nyali Beach walks
  • Local seafood dining experiences
  • Sunset dhow cruises (optional)

Important Notes

  • Prices are per person sharing (double occupancy)
  • Single room supplement available on request
  • Child rates available - contact us for details
  • Valid for travel 21st Dec 2025 - 3rd Jan 2026
  • Early booking essential - limited availability
  • Minimum stay requirements may apply
  • Peak season supplements included in pricing
