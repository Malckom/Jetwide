Recruitment

LAND THAT JOB

We provide tailored recruitment solutions designed to meet your specific needs.

Jetwide is a licensed recruitment agency in Kenya, accredited under the Labour Institutions Act (2007) and fully compliant with the Ministry of Labour, Chamber of Commerce, and respective embassies.

We specialize in overseas job placements for Kenyans in Serbia, UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Canada, USA, and Schengen countries. Whether professional or domestic, Jetwide is your trusted partner to secure safe, legal, and rewarding opportunities abroad.

Professional Jobs

Candidates will be required to:

  • Fill in formal applications & questionnaires
  • Attend interviews and group sessions
  • Provide confidential documents (CV, passport, police clearance, medical)
  • Pay agreed recruitment fees (for professional categories)
  • Sign legally binding contracts before deployment

Popular roles include: Drivers, Security, Hoteliers, Cleaners, Teachers, Accountants, Nurses, Doctors, Sales, and Marketing staff.

Domestic Jobs

  • Sponsored by the employer (no fees charged to candidates)
  • All documentation costs are covered by the employer
  • Typically 2-year contracts for domestic workers (house helps, caregivers)
  • Candidates must undergo medical exams and Home Care Training required by the Ministry of Labour

Documents Required

  • Police Clearance/Good conduct
  • Birth certificate
  • School Leaving Certificate (KCSE)
  • Valid Passport
  • Medical Report
  • Recommendation Letter
  • 2 colored Passport Size Photos
  • 1 colored full photo
  • Relevant Certificates/Diplomas

Countries Represented

  • Serbia
  • Saudi Arabia
  • UAE/GCC Countries
  • Canada
  • USA
  • Schengen Countries
