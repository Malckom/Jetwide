Detailed Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Kuala Lumpur
+

Highlights: Airport Arrival | Private Transfer | Hotel Check-in

  • Arrive at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA)
  • Meet and greet by our representative
  • Private transfer to your selected hotel in Kuala Lumpur
  • Check-in and room allocation (standard check-in: 3:00 PM)
  • Receive welcome pack with tour information
  • Rest of the day at leisure to relax after your journey
  • Evening Suggestions:
    • Explore nearby Bukit Bintang area
    • Visit Pavilion KL shopping mall

  • Overnight stay in Kuala Lumpur

Meals: None (arrival day)

Day 2
Genting Highlands Tour with Batu Caves Visit
+

Highlights: Batu Caves | Genting Highlands | Cable Car | Cool Mountain Climate

  • 8:00 AM: Breakfast at hotel
  • 9:00 AM: Depart for full-day Genting Highlands Tour
  • First Stop - Batu Caves:
    • Visit the iconic 140-foot golden statue of Lord Murugan
    • Climb the famous 272 colorful steps to the main cave temple
    • Explore the limestone cave formations and Hindu shrines
    • One of Malaysia's most renowned landmarks
    • Sacred Hindu site attracting devotees and tourists
    • Photo opportunities with resident monkeys
    • Duration: approximately 1 hour
  • Journey to Genting Highlands:
    • Scenic drive through lush tropical rainforest
    • Ascend to 6,000 feet above sea level
    • Notice temperature drop as you climb higher
  • Genting Highlands Experience:
    • Two-Way Cable Car Ride:
    • Board the Awana Skyway cable car
    • Enjoy spectacular panoramic views of tropical rainforest
    • Experience Southeast Asia's longest and fastest cable car
    • 10-minute scenic journey with breathtaking vistas
    • Arrive at Genting Highlands Resort
  • Free Time at Genting Highlands:
    • Enjoy the cool mountain climate (refreshing 18-22¬∞C)
    • Explore indoor and outdoor theme parks (optional)
    • Visit premium outlet stores for shopping
    • Try your luck at entertainment venues
    • Dine at various international restaurants
    • Take photos with scenic mountain backdrop
    • Duration: 2-3 hours free time
  • 5:30 PM: Return journey to Kuala Lumpur
  • 7:00 PM: Arrive at hotel
  • Evening at leisure
  • Overnight stay in Kuala Lumpur

Meals: Breakfast

Note: Bring a light jacket as Genting Highlands can be cool. Cable car operations are weather dependent.

Day 3
Kuala Lumpur City Tour + KL Tower Visit
+

Highlights: Private City Tour | KL Tower Observation Deck & Sky Deck | Major Landmarks

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • 9:00 AM: Depart for 5-hour Kuala Lumpur City Tour (private basis)
  • City Tour Highlights:
    • Independence Square (Dataran Merdeka):
      • Historic site where Malaysian flag was first raised
      • Colonial architecture and Sultan Abdul Samad Building
      • Photo opportunities with iconic buildings
    • National Monument:
      • Impressive bronze sculpture commemorating Malaysian heroes
      • Beautiful Lake Gardens setting
    • King's Palace (Istana Negara):
      • Official residence of Malaysian monarch
      • Photo stop at grand entrance
    • Petronas Twin Towers:
      • Photo stop at world's tallest twin towers
      • View from KLCC Park
      • Iconic 88-story buildings
    • Thean Hou Temple:
      • One of oldest and largest Chinese temples in Southeast Asia
      • Beautiful architecture and city views
    • Drive through Golden Triangle:
      • View modern Kuala Lumpur skyline
      • Shopping and business districts
  • Afternoon - KL Tower Visit:
    • Visit iconic Menara Kuala Lumpur (KL Tower)
    • 7th tallest telecommunications tower in the world
    • Observation Deck:
      • Located at 276 meters height
      • 360-degree panoramic views of Kuala Lumpur
      • Interactive displays and binoculars
    • Sky Deck (Open-air Platform):
      • Located at 300 meters - even higher views!
      • Open-air observation deck
      • Feel the breeze while enjoying spectacular vistas
      • Perfect for photography enthusiasts
    • Duration: 1-2 hours
  • 2:30 PM: Return to hotel
  • Rest of day at leisure for:
    • Shopping at nearby malls
    • Spa treatments
    • Exploring local markets
    • Relaxation at hotel
  • Overnight stay in Kuala Lumpur

Meals: Breakfast

Photography Tip: Both observation levels offer stunning views - bring your camera! Best photos from Sky Deck during golden hour.

Highlights: Coastal Escape | Dickson Dragon Sunset Cruise | Saltwater Jacuzzi | Beach Relaxation

  • Arrive at Port Dickson:
    • Short break for refreshments
    • Beach walk and relaxation
    • Photo opportunities along the coastline
  • Dickson Dragon Sunset Cruise Experience:
    • Board the Dickson Dragon cruise boat

    • Saltwater Jacuzzi on Board:
      • Relax in warm bubbling saltwater jacuzzi
      • Unique experience - jacuzzi while cruising!
      • Perfect for relaxation and photo opportunities
    • Sunset Cruise Highlights:
      • Sail along the calm Strait of Malacca waters
      • Watch spectacular sunset over the horizon
      • Enjoy cool sea breeze and tranquil atmosphere
      • Complimentary light refreshments
      • Background music creating perfect ambiance
      • Duration: approximately 2 hours
    • Capture beautiful sunset photographs
    • Unwind in jacuzzi while viewing coastal scenery
  • After Cruise:
    • Brief time at Port Dickson beach
    • Dinner at local restaurant (own expense)
  • Evening: Return journey to Kuala Lumpur hotel
  • 9:30 PM: Arrive at hotel
  • Overnight stay in Kuala Lumpur

Meals: Breakfast

What to Bring: Swimwear for jacuzzi, towel, camera, sunscreen, and light jacket for evening breeze. Cruise timing may vary based on sunset schedule.
