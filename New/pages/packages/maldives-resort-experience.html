Holiday Overview

Escape to paradise with our 6-day Maldives Resort Experience. Immerse yourself in the ultimate tropical luxury as you stay in stunning over-water or beachfront villas surrounded by crystal-clear turquoise lagoons, pristine white-sand beaches, and vibrant coral reefs.

The Maldives is the definition of a dream destination – an archipelago of 1,190 coral islands scattered across the Indian Ocean. Our carefully selected resorts offer the perfect combination of luxury accommodation, world-class dining, and unforgettable marine experiences. Whether you're celebrating a honeymoon, anniversary, or simply seeking relaxation and adventure, this package delivers the ultimate Maldivian escape with full-board dining included.

Experience Highlights

  • Stay in luxurious over-water villas with direct lagoon access or pristine beachfront bungalows
  • Enjoy full-board dining featuring international cuisine, fresh seafood, and Maldivian specialties
  • Snorkel in house reefs teeming with tropical fish, rays, and sea turtles just steps from your villa
  • Experience world-class diving at nearby sites (optional activity)
  • Relax with spa treatments overlooking the endless Indian Ocean
  • Witness spectacular sunsets from your private deck
  • Optional dolphin watching cruise through Maldivian waters
  • Complimentary non-motorized water sports including kayaking, paddleboarding, and windsurfing

Important Notes

  • Climate: The Maldives has a tropical monsoon climate. Best time to visit: November to April (dry season). Expect warm temperatures year-round (25-32°C).
  • Visa: Free 30-day tourist visa on arrival for most nationalities. Valid passport required with at least 6 months validity.
  • Currency: Maldivian Rufiyaa (MVR), but US Dollars are widely accepted and preferred at resorts. Credit cards accepted at all resorts.
  • Transfers: Speedboat or seaplane transfers from Male International Airport to your resort island (included). Seaplane transfers offer spectacular aerial views but operate only during daylight hours.
  • Alcohol: The Maldives is a Muslim country. Alcohol is only available at resort islands, not on inhabited local islands or in Male city.
  • Marine Life: Respect the delicate ecosystem. Do not touch coral, feed fish, or disturb marine life. Use reef-safe sunscreen only.
  • Activities: While full board is included, special excursions like diving, dolphin cruises, and spa treatments are at additional cost.
  • Dress Code: Resort casual. Modest dress required if visiting local inhabited islands.
  • Health: No mandatory vaccinations. Stay hydrated, use high SPF sunscreen, and be cautious of strong UV rays.

Your Maldives Experience

While each day in the Maldives is yours to enjoy at your own pace, here's a suggested itinerary to help you make the most of your tropical paradise escape.

Day 1

Arrival & Transfer to Paradise

Welcome to the Maldives! Your journey to paradise begins with arrival at Velana International Airport in Male, the capital of the Maldives.

After clearing customs and immigration (free visa stamp on arrival), you'll be greeted by your resort representative who will escort you to your transfer. Depending on your resort location, you'll travel by:

  • Speedboat Transfer (30-90 minutes) – Exciting ride across turquoise waters with the warm tropical breeze
  • Seaplane Transfer (15-45 minutes) – Spectacular aerial views of atolls, coral reefs, and resort islands from above (operates daylight hours only)

The journey to your resort is an experience in itself – you'll see countless islands dotting the endless blue ocean, some inhabited, many pristine and untouched.

Upon arrival at your resort island, receive a warm Maldivian welcome with refreshing drinks and cold towels. Complete check-in formalities while learning about resort facilities and available activities.

Settle into your luxurious villa – whether over-water with glass floor panels or beachfront with direct sand access. Step out onto your private deck and be mesmerized by the incredible views.

Enjoy your first dinner at the resort (included in full board) and watch the spectacular Maldivian sunset paint the sky in shades of orange, pink, and purple.

Accommodation: Selected resort

Meals: Dinner (if arrival is before dinner service)

Transfers: Airport to resort included

Day 2

Snorkeling & Beach Bliss

Wake up to the gentle sound of waves and enjoy breakfast at the main restaurant – fresh tropical fruits, pastries, made-to-order eggs, and ocean views.

Morning Activities:

  • Explore your resort's house reef – one of the Maldives' greatest treasures. Simply walk from the beach or down your over-water villa steps into a world of color
  • Snorkel among vibrant coral gardens, schools of tropical fish, parrotfish, butterflyfish, and if you're lucky, sea turtles and rays
  • Collect complimentary snorkeling gear from the dive center (available throughout your stay)
  • Attend a snorkeling briefing session if you're new to the activity

Afternoon Relaxation:

  • Lounge on the pristine white-sand beach with a good book
  • Swim in the crystal-clear lagoon
  • Try complimentary water sports: kayaking, paddleboarding, or windsurfing
  • Indulge in a spa treatment (optional, at extra cost) – massages in overwater pavilions with ocean views are unforgettable

Enjoy lunch at the main restaurant (full board included) featuring international and Asian cuisine.

Late afternoon is perfect for sunset activities – take photos from the pier, enjoy cocktails at the beach bar (drinks at own expense), or simply relax on your villa deck.

Dinner tonight might feature a themed buffet – expect fresh seafood, Maldivian curries, international favorites, and tropical desserts.

Accommodation: Selected resort

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner (full board)

Day 3

Marine Adventures & Island Exploration

Start your day with breakfast, then choose your adventure!

Suggested Morning Activity – Dolphin Watching Cruise (Optional):

Join a dolphin watching excursion (available at most resorts, approximately USD 50-75). Speed across the waves at sunrise or late afternoon to see:

  • Spinner dolphins performing acrobatic leaps
  • Bottlenose dolphins swimming alongside the boat
  • The vast expanse of the Indian Ocean stretching to the horizon
  • Possible sightings of flying fish, tuna, and other marine life

Alternative Morning Activities:

  • Scuba Diving – If you're certified, explore nearby dive sites with the resort dive center. The Maldives offers some of the world's best diving (optional, approximately USD 80-120 per dive)
  • Island Hopping – Visit a local inhabited island to experience Maldivian culture and shop for souvenirs (optional excursion)
  • Morning Snorkeling Session – Return to the house reef or join a guided snorkeling excursion to remote reefs

Afternoon:

Return for lunch at the resort. Spend the afternoon at your leisure:

  • Relax by the infinity pool
  • Enjoy more snorkeling – the fish are most active in early afternoon
  • Try fishing from your overwater villa deck
  • Take a cooking class (if offered by your resort)
  • Nap in a beach hammock swaying under palm trees

This evening, consider dining at the resort's specialty à la carte restaurant (included in full board at most resorts, or small supplement). Fresh grilled seafood, Asian fusion, or Italian cuisine with ocean views.

Accommodation: Selected resort

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner (full board)

Optional: Dolphin cruise or diving at extra cost

Day 4

Pure Relaxation & Water Activities

Today is all about embracing the Maldivian lifestyle – where time slows down and relaxation is the only agenda.

Morning:

  • Sleep in and enjoy a leisurely breakfast
  • Book a couples' massage at the spa (optional) – many resorts offer overwater spa pavilions with glass floor panels to watch fish while you relax
  • Attend a yoga session on the beach (complimentary at many resorts)
  • Snorkel a different section of the house reef to discover new marine life

Afternoon Adventures:

  • Try stand-up paddleboarding across the calm lagoon – the water is so clear you can see the sandy bottom
  • Take out a kayak and explore the coastline of your resort island
  • Join a sunset fishing trip (optional) – try traditional Maldivian line fishing and catch your dinner!
  • Visit the resort's marine center or attend a marine biology presentation to learn about coral conservation

Lunch and dinner included (full board). Tonight might feature a special beach barbecue or seafood grill evening.

After dinner, don't miss the opportunity for stargazing – the Maldives has minimal light pollution, and the night sky is spectacular. You can see the Milky Way clearly from your villa deck.

Pro tip: If you're celebrating a special occasion (honeymoon, anniversary), speak to the resort's guest services about arranging a private beach dinner (optional, supplement applies).

Accommodation: Selected resort

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner (full board)

Day 5

Final Full Day in Paradise

Make the most of your last full day in the Maldives!

Morning:

  • Take a final early morning snorkel to say goodbye to your fish friends
  • Capture sunrise photos from your villa or the beach
  • Enjoy a leisurely breakfast while soaking in the ocean views
  • If you haven't yet, try catamaran sailing (complimentary at many resorts)

Last-Minute Adventures:

  • Private Sandbank Visit (optional, approximately USD 100-150) – Be dropped on a deserted sandbank in the middle of the ocean for ultimate seclusion and photos
  • Final Spa Treatment – Book that massage you've been putting off
  • Snorkeling Excursion – Join a boat trip to distant reefs where manta rays gather (seasonal)
  • Shopping – Visit the resort boutique for souvenirs, jewelry, and resort wear

Afternoon:

Spend your final afternoon exactly as you wish – whether that's one more snorkeling session, lounging on the beach, floating in the infinity pool, or simply relaxing in your villa with the doors open to the ocean breeze.

Pack your bags this evening (but leave room for wet swimsuits and towels!).

For your final dinner, perhaps request a table on the beach or opt for in-villa dining to make the most of your last evening in paradise. Toast to an incredible holiday with a tropical cocktail as the sun sets.

Accommodation: Selected resort

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner (full board)

Day 6

Departure – Farewell to Paradise

Depending on your flight time and transfer schedule, you may have time for one last swim or breakfast at the resort.

Check out procedures will be explained the evening before. Complete check-out formalities and settle any extras (spa treatments, excursions, beverages, etc.).

Transfer back to Male International Airport:

  • Speedboat transfers can operate any time
  • Seaplane transfers operate during daylight hours only (approximately 6:00 AM to 4:00 PM)

If you have an evening flight and a morning seaplane transfer, you may have several hours in Male. The resort can arrange luggage storage and provide suggestions for activities in the capital:

  • Visit the Fish Market and Local Market
  • See the Friday Mosque (Grand Mosque)
  • Explore Sultan Park
  • Lunch at a local restaurant

Proceed to Velana International Airport for your departure flight to Nairobi.

Leave the Maldives with incredible memories of turquoise waters, vibrant marine life, luxurious relaxation, and the warmth of Maldivian hospitality. Dhanyavadh (thank you) and safe travels!

Meals: Breakfast (if time permits)

Transfers: Resort to airport included

Note: Consider extending your stay for a few more nights – we can arrange extensions at your current resort or transfer to another island for a different experience!
